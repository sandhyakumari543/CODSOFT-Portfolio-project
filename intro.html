About me

Hey there! I'm Sandhya Kumari, originally Pune. I recently graduated in 2023 with a BCA degree, securing an impressive 84.3%.

During my academic journey, I've not only excelled in studies but also explored the world of technology through practical experiences. I had the opportunity to intern in data analytics at CloudyML, where I honed my analytical skills and applied theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.

Besides academics and tech, I have a creative side. I've delved into various projects, including coding and web development. One of my notable projects is the portfolio you're currently exploring! It's a culmination of my skills, showcasing what I've learned and accomplished.

When I'm not coding or analyzing data, you can find me exploring new destinations. Traveling is my passion, and it allows me to experience diverse cultures, cuisines, and landscapes. I'm also an avid painter, always eager to put my creativity on canvas.

I believe in continuous learning, and that's not just limited to the technical realm. Learning new things, be it a coding language or a painting technique, excites me. It's this curiosity and passion that drive me to excel in every aspect of life.

Let's connect and create something amazing together! If you're interested in my portfolio, you're already on the right track. Feel free to explore my projects, and I'm open to any collaboration or feedback!
